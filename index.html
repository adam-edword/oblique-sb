To us, a company's image is one of the most important factors. That's why our approach at branding is taken from a different angle.

Design the Unexpected for the Passionate.

At Oblique, we take your purpose and make it our mission. We’ll find your angle with a fresh perspective, and we’ll give your business the edge it needs to get noticed.

A design agency based in Santa Barbara, CA, Oblique was built on the philosophy that every brand can help create lasting, positive change in the world. As for us, we’re in business to help you reach your goals and then use our profits to help the planet.

Your Story from A New Angle.

Oblique is more than website design and copy. We’re here to coach you through developing your brand—its vision, its story, its promise, and more. We want to hear your story so much that our initial consultation is free. Tell us what you’re all about.

Developing your brand helps us present it authentically to the world. We’ll design a gorgeous, website that reflects your vision and makes you look like the ultimate modern professional.

Earth First

It’s as simple as that. Yes, we absolutely love brand development, web & graphic design. And we really love helping our clients make a difference in their own unique ways.

But our truest love? The planet. It’s our home, and it’s the most beautiful home in the universe (plus, it’s the only one that sustains life. We’d like to keep living.). So, Oblique donates 5% of our total revenue towards science-based environmental projects.

When you work with Oblique, you’re helping the Earth, too. We’ll help you make your difference effectively, and you’ll help us support olivine beaches, carbon capture & offsets, reforestation, and sustainable agriculture that revitalizes the land.
